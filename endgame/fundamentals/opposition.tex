\section{Opposition}
\index{Opposition}

Opposition is the state of two kings standing 
on the same file with one square separating them
(``close'' opposition; three or five squares between is called ``distant''
opposition). The opposition may be vertical, horizontal or diagonal.

\index{Opposition}

The stronger side gets the opposition in order to execute an outflanking,
where the enemy king retreats to one side and our king then attacks the other way.
The weaker side gets the opposition in order to prevent this outflanking.
