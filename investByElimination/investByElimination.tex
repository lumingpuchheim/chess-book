\chapter{Enron 2000 Annual Report} \label{appendix:enron_2000_annual_report}

This appendix contains the complete text of Enron's 2000 Annual Report Letter to Shareholders and related sections. This document serves as a case study of how a company can present itself as successful while hiding fundamental problems. The report demonstrates many of the warning signs discussed in this book: vague language, focus on manipulable metrics, complex business models, and overconfidence.
